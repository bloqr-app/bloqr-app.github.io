Delete Your Data

Last updated: May 15, 2026

🗑️ Bloqr stores your blocking rules and settings locally on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data immediately. No action required on your end for local data.

What data Bloqr holds about you

Data type Where it lives How to delete Retention
Blocking rules, schedules, settings, PIN Your device only (MMKV local storage) Uninstall the app Instant on uninstall
App usage statistics Your device only Uninstall the app Instant on uninstall
Google account (Sign in with Google) Google / Supabase Email us (see below) Deleted within 30 days
Purchase / subscription history RevenueCat servers Email us (see below) Deleted within 30 days
Ad interaction data Google AdMob Google account settings Per Google's policy

How to delete all your data

1
Delete local data — uninstall Bloqr Go to Android Settings → Apps → Bloqr → Uninstall. All blocking rules, schedules, usage stats, and preferences stored on your device are permanently deleted immediately.
2
Delete your account and server-side data — send us an email If you used "Sign in with Google", we hold a minimal profile (email + user ID) in our authentication service. Send a deletion request to support@bloqr.app with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" from the Google account you used to sign in. We will delete your account and request removal from RevenueCat within 30 days and confirm by email.
3
Delete Google advertising data (optional) Bloqr uses Google AdMob in the free tier. To manage or reset your advertising ID, visit myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy or go to Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

What is retained and why

After processing your deletion request, the following may be retained for the periods indicated by law:

  • Transaction records — payment receipts and purchase events may be retained for up to 5 years to comply with applicable tax and accounting regulations. These records contain no behavioral data — only transaction amounts and dates.
  • Aggregated, anonymised analytics — if any aggregated crash or performance data was collected by third-party SDKs, it cannot be attributed back to you and is not subject to deletion.

Send a deletion request

Email us from the address associated with your Bloqr account. Include the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days.
